Ireland’s Most Dramatic New Lord of the Rings Fan Fiction

In the mist-soaked corners of Middle-earth nobody ever dared to imagine, one unlikely hero rises from the emerald shores of Éire itself... Julian Ironshirt — scholar, dreamer, accidental adventurer — finds himself tangled in legends older than stone, older than song, and older than even the oldest pub in Dublin.

“To Urlané!” is a mythic saga of courage, wandering hearts, mysterious green magic, and the stubborn hope of a people who refuse to vanish quietly. More than fan-fiction… it is a love letter to Tolkien, to Ireland, and to every storyteller who ever believed their world deserved a place in the legendarium.

Journey with Julian Ironshirt as he uncovers hidden histories, faces strange shadows, and lifts his voice in a cry that may yet echo across worlds:

TO URLANÉ!

This is only the beginning… stay tuned…
